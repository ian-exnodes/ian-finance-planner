# 07 — Future Ideas

These ideas are intentionally out of scope for v1.

Do not implement unless explicitly requested.

## Possible v2 Features

- Recurring bills with custom recurrence
- Savings goals
- Emergency fund tracking
- CSV/Excel import
- Export to PDF
- Push/email reminders
- OCR receipt or bill import
- AI spending analysis
- Budget categories
- Monthly reports
- Multi-currency
- Mobile native app
- PWA offline support

## Design Constraint

Do not overbuild v1 to support all future ideas.

Keep the architecture clean enough to extend, but prioritize current requirements.
